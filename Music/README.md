# Ric Steel - Music Career Archive

This folder contains materials related to Frederick "Ric" Stalnecker's 40+ year career as an international musician and performer under the stage name Ric Steel.

## Career Highlights

- **5,000+ live performances** worldwide
- **5 million air miles** as a touring musician
- **2 Billboard-charted singles:**
  - "The Radio Song" (#57)
  - "Whose Baby Are You" (#59)
- **20 years** headlining on 30+ cruise ships annually
- **BMI-accredited songwriter** with major cuts
- **Gold Medal** from Germany as Best Country Singer
- **Grammy nomination** for "Love, Work & Money"
- **Honorable mention** in John Lennon Songwriting Contest

## Notable Songwriting Credits

- Wrote for **The Oak Ridge Boys**
- **Lee Greenwood** recorded "Who's That Knocking on My Heart?" (on the "Wounded Heart" album featuring "God Bless The USA")
