## Key Messages

### What THEOS Is

**Runtime governance layer** that wraps AI systems (like Claude) to provide:
- Structured dialectical reasoning (dual engines + Governor)
- Safety mechanisms (contradiction budget, stop conditions, quarantine, tool control)
- Graceful degradation (refuses instead of failing catastrophically)
- Temporal continuity (accumulates wisdom across sessions)

### What's Validated

**Tested on Claude Sonnet 4.5:**
- 33% risk reduction (p < 0.001)
- 56% convergence improvement (p < 0.001)
- 10-15% quality increase (p < 0.05)
- 2.5-3.5x cost overhead (measured)

**Cross-platform validation:** 6 AI systems (Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT, Manus, Copilot, Perplexity)

**Needs:** Independent replication by Anthropic

### What We're Asking

**6-month collaboration:**
- Month 1-2: Independent validation (Anthropic replicates results)
- Month 3-4: Pilot deployment (medical diagnosis support)
- Month 5-6: Production readiness assessment

**Cost/Value:**
- **Anthropic investment:** ~$70K (compute + 2-3 researchers, 50% time, 6 months)
- **THEOS contribution:** ~$150K value (test suite, integration support, technical consultation)
- **Potential return:** $500K-$2M (differentiated product, regulatory framework, research leadership)

**Decision points:** Month 2, 4, 6 (clear success/fail criteria, off-ramps)

### What Anthropic Gets

**If successful:**
- Validated safety architecture for Claude
- Differentiated enterprise offering (high-stakes applications)
- Regulatory compliance framework (auditable reasoning)
- Research leadership (peer-reviewed publication)

**If unsuccessful:**
- Joint publication of findings (valuable for field)
- Minimal investment ($70K vs. potential $500K-$2M return)

### Why Now

**Timing is right:**
- THEOS has preliminary validation (ready for independent replication)
- Anthropic has Claude Sonnet 4.5 (validated platform)
- AI safety is priority (regulatory pressure, enterprise demand)
- Runtime governance is emerging field (opportunity for leadership)

## Addressing Perplexity's Feedback

This pitch package was refined based on detailed feedback from Perplexity AI. Key improvements:

✅ **"Tighten the front door"** - Executive Summary provides 2-3 page entry point

✅ **"Make validation artifacts easier to audit"** - Validation Appendix has single table with all empirical evidence

✅ **"Emphasize Claude-specific wins"** - All 4 governance mechanisms tested on Sonnet 4.5, concrete API patterns

✅ **"De-emphasize speculative numbers"** - Economic projections moved to appendices, focus on validated results

✅ **"Park Tier-3 architectures"** - Research Frontiers clearly labels Tier 1 (NOW) vs. Tier 2/3 (LATER)

✅ **"Concrete datasets and benchmarks"** - Validation Appendix pairs protocols with tasks, datasets, statistical significance

✅ **"Failure modes and negative results"** - Integration Sketch includes explicit failure scenarios and learnings

✅ **"Operational integration story"** - Integration Sketch provides concrete deployment example with rollout plan

✅ **"Lead with Overlay First"** - Executive Summary and 6-Month Proposal focus on validated overlay, native as future research bet
