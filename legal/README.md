## N.D.A. Overview

### When N.D.A. Is Required
- Detailed hardening corpus review
- Pilot deployment configurations
- Quantitative benchmark data
- Integration-specific guidance

### When N.D.A. Is NOT Required
- Public governance materials ([`/governance/`](../governance/))
- Academic research papers
- Reference implementation review
- General architecture discussions

### Standard N.D.A. Terms
- **Mutual:** Both parties protect confidential information
- **Duration:** 2 years from disclosure
- **Exclusions:** Publicly available information, independently developed

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I implement THEOS for my research project?
Yes, under the Academic/Research License. No fee required for non-commercial use.

### Can I deploy THEOS in production without a license?
No. Commercial deployment requires a separate licensing agreement.

### Can I modify THEOS for my specific use case?
Yes, under a commercial license. Modifications must maintain compliance with formal invariants.

### Can I contribute improvements back to THEOS?
Yes, under a contributor agreement. Contact us to discuss.

### What if I independently developed something similar?
Independent development is not restricted. However, if you reference THEOS materials, citation and licensing terms apply.
