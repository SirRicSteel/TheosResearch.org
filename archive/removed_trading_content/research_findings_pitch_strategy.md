## Deep Tech IP Licensing: Key Findings

### Typical Licensing Deal Structures

**Equity Stakes:**
- **U.S. Standard:** 5-15% equity for university spin-outs
- **UK Historical:** 20-30% (now trending down to 10-20%)
- **Stanford/Google Example:** 1.8M shares (became $336M+ at IPO)
- **Trend:** Single-digit to low-teens percentages are now considered "best practice"

**Royalty Rates:**
- **Software/AI:** 2-5% (low single digits)
- **Biotech/Pharma:** 3-7% (can be higher for blockbusters)
- **Hardware:** 2-4%
- **Note:** Royalties often structured as % of net revenue, not gross

**Upfront Fees:**
- Typically $10K-$100K for early-stage deep tech
- Covers patent filing costs and initial legal fees
- Often waived or deferred for founder-led spin-outs

**Milestone Payments:**
- Common in biotech (tied to FDA approvals, clinical trials)
- Less common in software/AI (harder to define clear milestones)
- Typical range: $50K-$500K per milestone

**Exclusivity:**
- **Exclusive licenses:** Required for most investor-backed startups
- **Non-exclusive:** Only viable for mature, revenue-generating companies
- **Field-of-use restrictions:** Common (e.g., "healthcare only" or "North America only")

### Key Lessons from Successful Deals

**Stanford/Google:**
- University took equity risk instead of high royalties
- Token royalty (~$400K/year) + massive equity upside
- Lesson: Equity can vastly outperform royalties for breakthrough tech

**AbCellera (UBC Spin-out):**
- Worldwide exclusive license
- Became top Canadian biotech, IPO'd successfully
- Lesson: Exclusive license critical for attracting industry partnerships

**Common Failure Mode:**
- Universities demanding 25%+ equity + high royalties
- Result: Founders can't attract VC funding
- Company dies, university earns nothing

### Critical Success Factors for Licensing Negotiations

1. **Moderate Equity:** 5-15% is investor-friendly
2. **Low Royalties:** 2-5% doesn't kill unit economics
3. **Exclusive Rights:** Essential for VC-backed startups
4. **Clear IP Ownership:** No ambiguity on inventorship
5. **Founder Motivation:** Enough equity left to stay committed

### For THEOS Licensing Strategy

**Recommended Structure:**

1. **Licensing Model:**
   - Offer **non-exclusive licenses** for research/academic use
   - Offer **exclusive field-of-use licenses** for commercial deployment (e.g., "healthcare AI" or "financial services AI")
   - Retain right to license to multiple non-competing verticals

2. **Pricing Tiers:**
   - **Tier 1 (Pilot/POC):** $50K upfront + 2% royalty on revenue
   - **Tier 2 (Enterprise Deployment):** $250K upfront + 3-5% royalty
   - **Tier 3 (Strategic Partnership):** Custom deal with equity component

3. **White-Label Option:**
   - Flat annual fee ($500K-$2M) for right to rebrand and deploy
   - No per-transaction royalties (simplifies accounting)
   - Includes technical support and updates

4. **Acquisition Terms:**
   - **Option A:** Full IP purchase ($5M-$15M based on validation)
   - **Option B:** Acqui-hire (IP + founder as employee, $3M-$10M + ongoing salary)
   - **Option C:** Strategic investment (equity stake + licensing rights)

5. **Humanitarian Clause:**
   - All proceeds directed to 508(c) charity
   - Opens access to corporate charitable budgets
   - Differentiates from pure profit-motivated deals

## AI Company Acquisitions: Key Due Diligence Requirements

### Five Critical AI-Specific Issues Buyers Evaluate

**1. IP Rights & Ownership**
- Clear ownership of all critical IP (patents, copyrights, trade secrets)
- Assignment agreements from all contributors (employees, contractors, research institutions)
- No third-party encumbrances on registered IP
- Waivers of moral rights from all contributors
- Open-source software audit (to avoid GPL contamination)
- Freedom-to-operate analysis

**2. Ownership & Voting Structure**
- Clear cap table with all shareholders identified
- Options, warrants, and convertible securities fully disclosed
- Shareholder approval rights understood
- Chain of title verified for all shares
- No hidden voting rights or dissolution triggers

**3. Data Rights & Personal Information**
- Data collection, usage, and transfer rights clearly documented
- Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws
- Consents for data transfer in place
- Privacy policies and data handling procedures audited
- Third-party data agreements reviewed

**4. Regulatory Risk & Life Cycle**
- Compliance with industry-specific regulations (FinTech, healthcare, etc.)
- Scalability and growth potential assessed
- Regulatory roadmap for future compliance
- Product life cycle and technical debt evaluated

**5. Cybersecurity**
- Data security measures audited by third-party experts
- Incident response plan in place
- Historical breach record reviewed
- Vulnerability assessment completed

### Deal Structure Strategies to Mitigate Risk

**Representations & Warranties:**
- IP ownership and freedom-to-operate
- Data compliance and consent adequacy
- Regulatory compliance
- Cybersecurity measures
- Financial statements accuracy

**Indemnification:**
- IP infringement claims
- Data breach liabilities
- Regulatory penalties
- Customer claims

**Escrow & Holdbacks:**
- 10-20% of purchase price held in escrow for 12-24 months
- Covers breach of reps & warranties
- Released upon successful integration

**Earnouts:**
- Performance-based payments (20-40% of total consideration)
- Tied to revenue, customer retention, or technical milestones
- Keeps founders motivated post-acquisition

**Acqui-Hire Structure:**
- Lower upfront payment for IP
- Higher ongoing compensation for founder as employee
- Retention bonuses tied to 2-4 year vesting
- Typical for early-stage or unproven tech

### Valuation Methods for AI Architecture IP

**Cost Approach:**

**Market Approach:**

**Income Approach:**
- Projected licensing revenue discounted to present value
- For THEOS: If 5 major labs license at $2M/year each = $10M annual revenue
- 10-year NPV at 15% discount rate ≈ $50M valuation

**Strategic Value:**
- Competitive advantage (first-mover in governed reasoning)

- Talent acquisition (founder expertise)

**Realistic Range for THEOS:**
