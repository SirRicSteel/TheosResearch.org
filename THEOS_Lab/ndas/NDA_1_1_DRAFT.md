Effective Date: ____________________

## 2. Purpose
The parties wish to exchange certain Confidential Information for the purpose of evaluating THEOS governance architecture, related materials, and potential research, licensing, or collaboration.

## 3. Confidential Information
“Confidential Information” includes non-public information disclosed in any form, including:
- technical designs, code, experimental artifacts, research notes, and protocols
- business plans, pricing, licensing terms, and strategy
- any materials marked confidential or reasonably understood to be confidential

Confidential Information does **not** include information that:
(a) is publicly available through no breach,  
(b) was already known to the receiving party,  
(c) is independently developed without use of Confidential Information, or  
(d) is rightfully obtained from a third party without duty of confidentiality.

## 4. Obligations
Each party agrees to:
- use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose
- not disclose Confidential Information to third parties without written consent
- protect Confidential Information using at least reasonable care
- limit disclosure to employees/contractors with a need to know and who are bound by confidentiality

## 5. No License / No Transfer
No rights or licenses are granted by this Agreement except the limited right to evaluate for the Purpose. All intellectual property remains with the respective owner.

## 6. Term
This Agreement lasts **3 years** from the Effective Date. Confidentiality obligations survive for **3 years** after disclosure.

## 7. Return / Destruction
Upon request, the receiving party will return or destroy Confidential Information, except archival copies required for compliance.

## 8. Remedies
Unauthorized disclosure may cause irreparable harm; the disclosing party may seek injunctive relief in addition to other remedies.
