## The Concept

This book organizes AI knowledge by life stage, showing readers exactly how AI fits into THEIR world:
- Teenagers & High School Students
- College Students
- Young Professionals
- Parents & Families
- Entrepreneurs & Small Business Owners
- Seniors & Retirees

Each chapter provides practical applications, example prompts, and real-world case studies specific to that life stage.
