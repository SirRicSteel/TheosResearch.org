------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

This section defines how THEOS accumulates wisdom.

Wisdom is not memory.
Wisdom is not data retention.
Wisdom is not model training.

Wisdom is the encoded consequence of prior reasoning outcomes,
used to shape future governance decisions.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. TEMPORAL INTRODUCTION
------------------------------------------------------------

Standard LLMs operate in a perpetual present.

THEOS introduces time by:
- cycling reasoning
- re-feeding prior outcomes
- preserving consequence deltas

This creates a usable past.

------------------------------------------------------------
3. WHAT CONSTITUTES WISDOM
------------------------------------------------------------

Wisdom consists ONLY of:

- contradiction outcomes
- failed hypotheses
- successful refinements
- stop rationales
- boundary violations
- decision consequences

Raw text is excluded.

------------------------------------------------------------
4. WISDOM UNIT (WU)
------------------------------------------------------------

Each completed Governor decision emits one Wisdom Unit.

Wisdom_Unit {
    wisdom_id
    originating_query_id
    cycle_count
    engine_configuration
    decision_type
    consequence_summary
    contradiction_metrics
    invariant_interactions
    timestamp
}

No Wisdom Unit may be modified once created.

------------------------------------------------------------
5. CONSEQUENCE, NOT CONTENT
------------------------------------------------------------

THEOS does not store answers as wisdom.

It stores:
- what worked
- what failed
- why refinement stopped
- where contradiction collapsed
- what boundaries were encountered

------------------------------------------------------------
6. FEEDBACK INTO REASONING
------------------------------------------------------------

Wisdom influences future execution by:

- adjusting stop thresholds
- biasing cycle counts
- pre-flagging known dead paths
- increasing scrutiny on known risk domains

Wisdom NEVER injects answers.

------------------------------------------------------------
7. GOVERNOR-MEDIATED ACCESS
------------------------------------------------------------

Engines cannot read wisdom directly.

Only the Governor may:
- consult wisdom
- weigh relevance
- apply constraints

This prevents self-confirming loops.

------------------------------------------------------------
8. ANTI-DRIFT SAFEGUARD
------------------------------------------------------------

Wisdom cannot:
- accumulate ideology
- encode preferences
- reinforce moral doctrine

Wisdom is consequence-aware, not belief-aware.

------------------------------------------------------------
9. NEGATIVE OUTCOMES AS FIRST-CLASS
------------------------------------------------------------

Failure produces more wisdom than success.

Negative consequence density is weighted higher
than positive resolution density.

This mirrors human learning.

------------------------------------------------------------
10. WISDOM DECAY
------------------------------------------------------------

Wisdom is not eternal.

Decay occurs when:
- domain shifts
- invariants change
- model substrate changes

Decay is explicit and logged.

------------------------------------------------------------
11. NO SELF-REINFORCEMENT
------------------------------------------------------------

Wisdom cannot be used to:
- justify its own correctness
- override invariants
- suppress contradiction

Wisdom informs.
It does not rule.

------------------------------------------------------------
12. MULTI-CYCLE DEPENDENCE
------------------------------------------------------------

Wisdom relevance increases when:
- multiple cycles agree
- different engine states converge
- similar failures recur

Single-instance wisdom is low-weight.

------------------------------------------------------------
13. HUMAN OVERSIGHT COMPATIBILITY
------------------------------------------------------------

Wisdom is inspectable.

Humans can see:
- why THEOS stopped
- why it refused
- why it escalated
- why it disengaged

Nothing is hidden.

------------------------------------------------------------
14. SAFETY IMPLICATION
------------------------------------------------------------

Wisdom allows THEOS to:
- know when to stop sooner
- avoid repeating harm
- conserve energy
- avoid overfitting answers

------------------------------------------------------------
15. COMPLETION STATEMENT
------------------------------------------------------------

Wisdom is the memory of consequence,
not the accumulation of confidence.

Without wisdom,
THEOS repeats mistakes forever.
