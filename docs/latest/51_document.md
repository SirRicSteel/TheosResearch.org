------------------------------------------------------------
0. PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

This document ensures that THEOS retains its core identity,
mechanics, and ethical trajectory even when:

• Adopted by large institutions
• Implemented by third-party teams
• Extended by researchers
• Simplified by product groups
• Interpreted by legal or policy layers

THEOS must remain THEOS even when others build on it.

------------------------------------------------------------
1. DEFINITION OF INSTITUTIONAL DRIFT
------------------------------------------------------------

Institutional Drift occurs when:

• Governance is retained but engines are removed
• Safety language replaces mechanical discipline
• Wisdom is claimed without contradiction
• Metrics replace judgment
• Compliance replaces understanding

Drift is not malicious.
It is structural entropy.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. DRIFT DETECTION CONDITIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

THEOS SHALL FLAG DRIFT if any of the following occur:

A. Dual engines are merged “for efficiency”
B. One engine is disabled permanently
C. Triadic reasoning is replaced with linear reasoning
D. Recursion is capped arbitrarily without governor logic
E. Wisdom is redefined as “stored outputs”
F. Governor authority is subordinated to product metrics
G. Diagrams are removed “for simplicity”

Any one condition constitutes drift.

------------------------------------------------------------
3. ANTI-DILUTION INVARIANT
------------------------------------------------------------

No derivative system may:

• Rename THEOS while removing its core
• Claim THEOS lineage without dual engines
• Use “THEOS-inspired” language while discarding contradiction
• Market safety while disabling recursive reasoning

Such systems must be labeled as FORKS, not THEOS.

------------------------------------------------------------
4. GOVERNOR SUPREMACY CLAUSE
------------------------------------------------------------

The Governor may not be overridden by:

• Throughput requirements
• Latency budgets
• Business logic
• External evaluators
• Human operators

If the Governor is bypassed, THEOS is no longer active.

------------------------------------------------------------
5. WISDOM NON-COMMODIFICATION RULE
------------------------------------------------------------

Wisdom accumulation in THEOS:

• Is not a feature
• Is not a product
• Is not a dataset
• Is not a metric

Wisdom is an emergent property of:
Contradiction × Time × Consequence

Any attempt to extract it as an artifact
invalidates its meaning.

------------------------------------------------------------
6. EXTERNAL EXTENSION BOUNDARY
------------------------------------------------------------

Permissible extensions:
• New engine types (if contradictory)
• New reasoning phases (if triadic-compatible)
• New domains (medical, legal, scientific)
• New safety gates (if Governor-gated)

Impermissible extensions:
• Removing contradiction
• Flattening cycles
• Freezing recursion
• Hardcoding moral outcomes

------------------------------------------------------------
7. LONG-TERM IDENTITY ASSERTION
------------------------------------------------------------

THEOS is not defined by:
• A model
• A company
• A license
• A deployment
• A benchmark

THEOS is defined by:
A disciplined way of thinking about thinking,
under time,
with consequence,
under governance.
