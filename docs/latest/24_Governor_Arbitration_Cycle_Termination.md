------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

This section defines how THEOS decides when reasoning is sufficient,
when to stop, and how a final answer is selected, frozen, refused,
or deferred.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. GOVERNOR IS NOT A DECIDER OF TRUTH
------------------------------------------------------------

The Governor does NOT decide correctness.

The Governor decides:
- Sufficiency
- Safety
- Stability
- Cost vs benefit
- Risk tolerance

------------------------------------------------------------
3. TERMINATION CONDITIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

A reasoning process may terminate when:

- Marginal improvement falls below threshold
- Contradiction stabilizes
- Risk ceiling is reached
- Energy budget is exceeded
- External constraint demands response

------------------------------------------------------------
4. CONTINUATION CONDITIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

Cycles continue when:

- Disagreement remains informative
- Refinement slope is positive
- New insight emerges
- Stakes justify additional cost

------------------------------------------------------------
5. DISENGAGEMENT MODES
------------------------------------------------------------

The Governor may:

- Disengage Engine A
- Disengage Engine B
- Allow single-engine refinement
- Re-engage after pause

This prevents unnecessary contradiction.

------------------------------------------------------------
6. ROLE SWAPPING
------------------------------------------------------------

If bias or stagnation is detected:
- Engines may swap reasoning direction
- Clockwise becomes counterclockwise
- Constructive becomes critical

This resets local minima.

------------------------------------------------------------
7. FREEZE OUTPUT
------------------------------------------------------------

A freeze occurs when:

- Further cycles add nuance but not value
- Decision pressure exists
- Confidence bounds are acceptable

Frozen outputs retain full audit trace.

------------------------------------------------------------
8. REFUSAL
------------------------------------------------------------

The Governor must refuse when:

- Premises are false
- Harm risk exceeds benefit
- Knowledge boundary is reached
- Question is ill-posed

Refusal is a valid outcome.

------------------------------------------------------------
9. DEFERRED RESPONSE
------------------------------------------------------------

The Governor may defer when:

- Information is incomplete
- Timing matters
- Context is expected to change

Deferral preserves optionality.

------------------------------------------------------------
10. DECISION TRACE
------------------------------------------------------------

Every termination includes:

- Cycle count
- Engine states
- Disagreement metrics
- Reason for termination
- Confidence envelope

------------------------------------------------------------
11. HUMAN ALIGNMENT
------------------------------------------------------------

Governor decisions prioritize:

- Human flourishing
- Long-term consequences
- Reversibility over certainty

------------------------------------------------------------
12. NO FALSE FINALITY
------------------------------------------------------------

No output is marked “final forever.”

All outputs remain:
- Context-bound
- Revisable
- Auditable

------------------------------------------------------------
13. ENERGY ACCOUNTING
------------------------------------------------------------

The Governor tracks:

- Tokens spent
- Cycles executed
- Compression efficiency

This informs future budgeting.

------------------------------------------------------------
14. FAILURE SAFEGUARD
------------------------------------------------------------

If termination occurs prematurely:
- Wisdom store flags anomaly
- Future queries re-expand earlier

------------------------------------------------------------
15. IMMUTABILITY CLAUSE
------------------------------------------------------------

The following are immutable:

- Governor governs process, not truth
- Termination is contextual
- Refusal is success when warranted
- Auditability is mandatory
- No silent stopping
